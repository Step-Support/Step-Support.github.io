Our Process

  1. After you contact us and let us know what kind of condition your pet has, we'll begin drafting prosthetics and schedule an initial meetup to take measurements
  2. We'll begin to work on a prototype. within the next few weeks, we'll ask to meet up 2-3 more times for fittings.
  3. Once we finish perfecting our prototype, we'll begin to build the final model.
  4. If for any reason you are not satisfied with the results, or you encounter any problems with the prosthetic, you're more than welcome to contact us again to get it fixed :)
